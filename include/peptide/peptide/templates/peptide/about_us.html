About Us

Our Research

The Milk Bioactive Peptide Database (MBPDB) is a comprehensive platform dedicated to the exploration and understanding of bioactive peptides derived from milk proteins. As milk proteins undergo processing and digestion, they yield peptides with a spectrum of biological properties, ranging from antimicrobial and antioxidant to opioid and immunomodulatory effects. While numerous reviews have touched upon these attributes, the specifics about each peptide and its function were distributed across myriad research articles.
Our website consolidates this scattered information, offering users a centralized resource. Born from an extensive literature review, the MBPDB captures data on bioactive peptides from mammalian milk proteins. Beyond just listing, the platform offers visual mapping of these peptides on their parent protein sequences, spotlighting areas with a high concentration of bioactive peptides.
Furthermore, the site serves as a testament to the evolution of research in this field. Our team has delved deep into literature spanning decades, ensuring the MBPDB remains updated and relevant. This platform presents an exhaustive overview of research across various functional categories, from in vitro experiments to animal and clinical studies. By making such knowledge accessible, we aim to propel future research into the myriad benefits of milk peptides.
